Mail-link bookmarklet

Install this bookmarklet to create a mail-link for the email you are currently viewing in your webmail — with a single click, no copy-pasting.

Supported webmail: GMail (web). More may be added later.

×

Drag this button onto your bookmarks toolbar:

📧 Mail-Link

How to use

  1. Open an email in GMail (web).
  2. Click the Mail-Link bookmark.
  3. If several messages are open, pick one; then copy the link from the dialog.

Nothing is sent to any server; the link is built entirely in your browser.
